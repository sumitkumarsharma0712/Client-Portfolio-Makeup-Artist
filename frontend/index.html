The Artist Behind the Brush

Anjani is a professional makeup artist with a passion for enhancing natural beauty and a flair for high‑fashion editorial aesthetics. Every face is her canvas, and every look tells a bold, beautiful story. From bridal transformations to editorial shoots, she blends creativity with precision to deliver bespoke makeup experiences that celebrate individuality and confidence.

2024 - The Beginning

  • Entered the world of makeup artistry through dedicated training and practice.
  • Mastered the fundamentals of skin preparation and advanced product application through professional training at Queen Academy.
  • Built a strong foundation in natural beauty enhancement and professional techniques.

2025 - Bridal Specialist

  • Transitioned from learner to professional makeup artist.
  • Launched a signature bridal makeup studio, serving hundreds of brides and grooms.
  • Specialized in timeless, elegant bridal looks that became her hallmark.
  • Expanded into diverse creative projects, gaining recognition for precision and artistry.

Present - Editorial Pro

  • Established as a trusted professional makeup artist for weddings, editorial shoots, and personal transformations.
  • Known for crafting bold, unforgettable looks that merge fashion with individuality.
  • Continues to evolve her artistry, blending creativity with technical mastery.
  • Focuses on delivering bespoke experiences that empower clients with confidence and elegance.
